Usage Guidelines

Color Strategy

  • Grey carries 80% of the interface
  • Color infusions guide specific actions
  • Blue for primary actions and navigation
  • Orange for warnings and attention
  • Green for success and completion
  • Red reserved for errors only

Industrial Principles

  • High contrast for visibility
  • Large touch targets (44px minimum)
  • Monospace for data/codes
  • Clear visual hierarchy
  • Functional before decorative
  • Works in harsh lighting
